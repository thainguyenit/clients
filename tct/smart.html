Case Study

E-card application system

Customer
A Japanese company
Customer Needs
Card brand addition and different functions customize of the E-card application system will be implemented.
Our Solutions & Services
In the e-card application system of a credit card issuing company, we implement various functions for enhancement of security measures such as: Anti-Criminal Proceeds Act or improvement according to the related card brand addition.
Our company always perform multi short-term projects at once corresponding to each brand according to the specific projects. Consequently, close communication with japan side is required. In particular, we also share related issues as well as progress situation via teleconference three times a week.
Furthermore, we run the test by using auto-test tool for improving the work efficiently.
Responsible process：detail design, coding, UT test specs creation and perform UT.
Development scale
120 Man-months
Technical
C#, MVVM
