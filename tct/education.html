With the target to become the 200-employee offshore center and the model in order to build other ones in Southeast Asia, TCT Vietnam has built an organization with a detail hierachy to have a proper personel mechanism that improves employees' abilities and shows them how they are able to contribute and improve themselves when joining in TCT Vietnam.
We believe that the way to success will be shorter if we know directly how to go. We support our employees to realize their career path in detail.


Our training objective is to improve a professional competence of all employees and company as well. The theory of our in training is that the more mature our employees are, the more mature our company is.

Activities

Realizing that the company training activities are necessary, however they are not enough to improve our employees ability, TCT Vietnam has built another one in order to encourage employees to improve their skill by themselves such as doing seminar and e-learning.

Seminar activies encourage our employees to improve not only the technical skills but also other skills such as research skills, document writing skills, presentaion skills, ect.

The target of Trancosmos Technologies Inc is becoming a Global company so that we has at first realized the challenges of communication among the countries in near future. Therefore, beside improving Japanese skills for our employee to work with Japanese cusomers, we has also encourage our members to impove their English by themselves so that all of our members are able to use English in working in an international environment.
