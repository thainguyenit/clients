NGUYEN THANH HAI
Senior Software Engineer

NGUYEN THANH HAI

Senior Software Engineer

I have been working as a Senior Engineer at TCT-VN for nearly 1 year.
TCT-VN provides me a lot of opportunities to improve both technical skills and soft skills. There are many challenging projects from Japan side and internal seminars which I can study and strengthen my software development skills. In addition, I have chances to perform my leadership and management skills when I take the role team leader in TCT-VN.
Moreover, TCT-VN is like a family. The colleagues in TCT-VN are like brothers and sisters. Together we build the comfortable environment where we can work and enjoy our jobs. Besides, there are many activities which we can play and enjoy life together such as sport activities, beer party, company trip…
Finally, TCT-VN is a home where I can learn, work, play and enjoy my jobs as well as my life.
